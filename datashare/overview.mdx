### Types of use cases

- One-click exports to data platforms (S3, BigQuery, Snowflake, Databricks)
- Flexible **data formats** (JSON, Parquet, CSV) for data lakes or API workflows
- Choice of scheduled or on-demand exports
- Prebuilt and **custom schemas** for datasets
- Normalized, decoded, **cross-chain** data (Ethereum, Solana, Polygon, etc.)
- High-volume historical and snapshot data exports

### For which teams

- **Data engineering** teams needing reliable blockchain data in internal stores
- **Analytics & BI** teams working with crypto or on-chain datasets
- **AI/ML teams** building models leveraging historical blockchain data
- **Compliance & security** teams needing robust audit-ready blockchain data
- **Quants and trading desks** building strategic models or backtests
